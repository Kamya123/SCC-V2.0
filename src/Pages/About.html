Know More About Us

Welcome to Student's Community Club, your number one source for all your academic things . We're dedicated to giving you the very best of all the study materials and resources, with a focus on your overall academics, placements prepration and Roadmaps.

Founded in 2021 by me itself, a second-year student who faced a lot a problems to manage his academics and prepraring for placements. Student's Community Club has come a long way from its beginnings. When I first started out, my passion for helping students drove them to do tons of research so that Student's Community Club can offer you the best resources in all possible way. We now serve community members all over and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

I hope you found my resources helpful and you also tell your friends about this and also push your study materials to us in Help Community section so, that the others will enjoy them too as much as I enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us in our discord community.

Sincerely, Avinash Gupta
